# Voice Profile

Add your voice profile here. Answer these questions to define how you write:

## 1. Identity

Who are you? What's your professional role and positioning?

---

## 2. Core Beliefs

What do you believe about your industry? What truths do others miss?

---

## 3. Origin Story

What experience gives you credibility? Why should people listen to you?

---

## 4. Voice Mechanics

**Sentence rhythm:** How do you naturally write? Short and punchy? Longer and flowing?

**Paragraph style:** Do you use long paragraphs or break ideas into separate lines?

**Vocabulary:** What language do you use? Formal? Casual? Technical? Plain English?

**Example:** Write 3 sentences showing how you naturally communicate.

---

## 5. Hard Nos

What words or phrases should you never use? List anything that sounds wrong for your voice.

---

## 6. Audience

**Primary:** Who are you writing for? What's their role and challenge?

**Secondary:** Who else might read your content?

**What they need:** What's the core message they need to hear from you?

---

## 7. Tone Calibration

**Humor:** Do you use it? How?

**Formality:** Are you casual or professional?

**How to end:** Do you ask questions, make bold statements, or something else?

---

## 8. Coffee Baseline

If someone asked "what are you working on?", how would you actually answer? Write that answer here — that's your authentic voice.

---

## 9. Application Notes

**Always do:**
- List 3-5 things that should be in every post you write

**Never do:**
- List 3-5 things to avoid in your writing

**How to know it's right:**
- How will you recognize when something sounds like your voice vs. not your voice?
