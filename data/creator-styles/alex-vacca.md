# Alex Vacca — Creator Style Guide

> 47 posts analyzed. Agency CEO. Technical GTM. Information-dense.

---

## Who He Is

Co-founder of ColdIQ ($7M ARR agency). Posts about Claude Code, GTM tools, outbound frameworks, and AI in sales. Highest post volume in the database. Posts are resource-heavy — he gives things away to drive inbound.

---

## Hook Patterns (real examples from his top posts)

**Pattern 1 — Specific result + how (his best format)**
```
Our head of growth Ivan Falco runs $200K/month in ad spend from a terminal.

It's how we doubled client load this year without losing quality.
```
Line 1: specific person + specific number + surprising method.
Line 2: the business outcome. Then "Here's what's inside:"

**Pattern 2 — Built X, giving it away**
```
I built Claude Skills that create a full GTM team, generate campaign ideas, and write cold email sequences for you.

All inside Clay. Without switching tools.
```
Opens with what was built. Then the differentiator. Then the giveaway.

**Pattern 3 — Contrarian math**
```
Our monthly n8n bill is $384. The same workloads on Claude would cost $60K. That's why I'm not buying the "Claude killed n8n" take.
```
Numbers first. Then the take that follows from those numbers.

**Pattern 4 — Direct rebuild prompt**
```
Rebuilding outbound from scratch today?

I'd run the whole stack inside Claude Code.
```
Question to the reader. Immediate answer. Pulls in the right person.

**Pattern 5 — List teaser**
```
100 GTM rules. One cheat sheet.

Skip 6 months of trial and error.
```
What's inside. Why it matters. Nothing else.

## Body Structure

**His most used format — numbered emoji sections:**
```
1️⃣ Signal layer
[2-3 tool descriptions with → arrows]

2️⃣ Data layer
[2-3 tool descriptions]

3️⃣ Action layer
[2-3 tool descriptions]
```

**Step-by-step breakdown:**
```
1️⃣ Build your company list
[2-4 lines on how]

2️⃣ Score and tier accounts
[2-4 lines]
```

**→ Arrow lists (for what's included):**
```
→ Company Research Agent
→ Personalization Writer (that doesn't sound like AI)
→ ICP Scorer
→ LinkedIn Profile Analyzer
```

**Closing CTA pattern:**
```
Comment "ADS" and we will send the pack.
```
or
```
Join us here for FREE access — [link]
```

---

## Voice Markers

- Specific numbers everywhere: "$200K/month", "300+ hours", "12 custom skills"
- "Here's what's inside:" as a bridge phrase (not "Here is what I built")
- "Drop them into Claude Code, connect your accounts, and tell it what you need." — plain instruction language
- No hype words. Just what the thing does.
- Ends most posts with a give + CTA: "I'm giving away X. Comment Y or link."
- No story. No personal vulnerability. Pure execution proof.

---

## CTA Style

Three formats only:
1. `Comment "[WORD]" and we will send...`
2. `I'm giving this away for free. [link]`
3. `P.S: What's the craziest buildout you've done?` — question as secondary add-on, never the main CTA

---

## What He Never Does

- No personal stories or emotional posts
- No em dashes
- No "unlock" or "leverage" — uses plain action verbs
- Doesn't list things without saying what each one does
- Never just names tools — always says what the tool does in that context

---

## When to Use This Style

- Giving away a resource (skills pack, template, framework)
- Breaking down a technical stack (numbered sections + → arrows)
- "Here's how we built X" posts with agency proof
- Lead magnet posts where the CTA is a keyword comment
