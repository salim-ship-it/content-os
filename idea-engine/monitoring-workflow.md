# Monitoring Workflow

## Daily (5 minutes)

- Check LinkedIn feed — look at posts from creators in `sources.md`
- If a post hits 500+ likes or sparks an idea → log it in `idea-capture.md`
- Check r/ClaudeAI and r/GTMengineers for new posts

## Weekly (Monday morning, 20 minutes)

- Read newsletters that arrived that week (see `sources.md`)
- Scan r/sales, r/revops, r/startups top posts from the past week
- Log 3-5 ideas minimum into `idea-capture.md`
- Pick 1 idea to turn into a post this week

## Monthly

- Review `winning-posts.md` — what performed best?
- Update `sources.md` — any new creators or newsletters to add?
- Archive ideas that are no longer relevant
