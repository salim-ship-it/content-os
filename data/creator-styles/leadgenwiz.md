```
[Closing line on the key insight]

Comment "[WORD]" to get the full playbook.
```

---

## Voice Markers

- "No X. No Y. Just Z." — strips away complexity to one thing
- → arrows for the system steps
- Specific numbers always: "94 calls", "last month" — not "almost 100"
- "exact system" — the word "exact" signals it's not vague advice
- Short steps with the why included: "→ Step — because this"

---

## CTA Style

**Comment trigger:**
```
Comment "CALLS" and I'll send you the full system breakdown.
```

---

## What He Never Does

- No em dashes
- No motivational framing
- No vague outcomes ("more leads", "better results")
- Always specific

---

## When to Use This Style

- LinkedIn outreach result posts
- "Here's the exact system" breakdowns
- Posts where the outcome is the proof and the system is the offer
